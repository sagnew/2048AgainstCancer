Meet Tyler Wiley. He has been a great friend of mine since I was two years old. He was diagnosed with Medulloblastoma, a form of cancer that attacks the brain and spinal cord, at the age of 13. I wanted to figure out a way to help him raise money for a life saving clinical trial to treat his brain crancer, so my friend Faiq and I hacked together this new version of the popular addicting game, 2048. Log in with Venmo in order to play, and you can donate one cent for every pair of tiles that you merge! If you do not have Venmo, you can donate directly as well. We need all of the help we can get, and time is running out!
